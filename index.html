4 · How the maths works

The guide bush rides against the template edge while the cutter spins inside it. The cut line is therefore offset from the template edge. Only two formulas exist:

  • Offset = (Bush − Cutter) / 2 — when the piece you keep is on the same side as the bush (female→hole, male→plug).
  • Offset = (Bush + Cutter) / 2 — when the cutter works beyond the bush footprint (female→plug, male→hole).

Worked example (from the source chart): a 60 mm circular template with an 8 mm offset pair. Hole: 8 mm cutter + 24 mm bush → offset (24−8)/2 = 8 → hole Ø = 60 − 2×8 = 44 mm. Plug: 6 mm cutter + 10 mm bush → offset (10+6)/2 = 8 → plug Ø = 60 − 2×8 = 44 mm. A perfect-fit inlay.
